System Highlights
  • Full CRUD on products: add, search (by ID/name/SN), edit, and delete (with order-record guard).
  • Stock is read-only during product editing - updated exclusively through inbound/outbound orders.
  • Inbound orders from suppliers automatically increase product stock on creation/edit; decrease on deletion.
  • Outbound orders to buyers decrease stock (insufficient-stock check enforced); deletion restores stock.
  • Category management: add, edit, and delete categories (with product-reference guard).
  • Customer management: full CRUD for suppliers and buyers (with order-reference guard on delete).
  • Stall management: add, edit, activate/deactivate stalls with owner assignment.
  • Customers can browse products, manage favourites, and chat with stall owners.
